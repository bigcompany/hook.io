Free Development Accounts

You can sign up instantly for a Free Development Account. This is a great way to get started with microservices.

Get all the core features for free. Ideal for development and minimal traffic microservices.

Limitations

  • 1,000 Microservice Requests Per Month
  • No Private Microservices
  • Limited to 10 Log entries per Microservice
  • Limited to 100 Cloud Datastore documents
  • 15 second time-outs on all Microservice Requests
Click for a message about circumventing Free Account Limitations

If you aren't able to pay for an account and you intend to take the time to try and do something clever to bypass any account limitations, please just email us instead and we'll give you a free account.

Why? Believe it or not, one of the primary technical goals of this platform is to minimize electrical usage. We care about the planet. If you abuse the free account API, you are most likely unintentionally wasting CPU and RAM.

If you feel a specific limitation should be adjusted or tweaked, please just let us know and we'll do our best to fix it.
